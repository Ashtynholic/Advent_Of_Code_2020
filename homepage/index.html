So, Who is this lady?

Hi there! My name is Ashton Hollick, and I'm a student currently taking CS50 through Harvard Online. Im 37, a mother of two incredible boys (Billy, 10 years old, and Bobby, 3 years old). I've been married for over 18 years, and still going strong. I've been a musician and entertainer since I was 16 years old! I've opened up for bands like Candlebox, Queensryche, Stryper, and more! I grew up with a father that worked in tech fields, but both of my parents were musicians and taught me instruments and music theory together. At one point, I played in a band with my mother and sister, kinda like 'The Partridge Family' meets 'The Osbournes'.
